Definition
School Name The school's name is recorded in blue letters, signifying systematic management. The background of the school name in yellow signifies excellence in all fields.
Circular Shape The circle symbolizes global presence. SMK Kota Kemuning aims to be a renowned international school. Students here will also become globally competent.
Yellow Color Yellow signifies sovereignty, obedience, and loyalty. SMK Kota Kemuning has leaders at the highest and mid-levels and student leadership. SMK KK members are obedient and loyal to their leaders, ultimately empowering the nation.
Blue Color Blue signifies unity among students, teachers, parents, and the community. This unity transcends racial, religious, and national differences.
Red Color Red signifies courage. The SMK Kota Kemuning community courageously initiates changes for the betterment of the school. They also bravely express opinions, ideas, and views to enhance school performance.
